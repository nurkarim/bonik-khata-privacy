## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Bonik Khata ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application ("App").

**Important:** Bonik Khata is an **offline-first** personal finance management application. The vast majority of your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to use optional cloud backup features.

By using Bonik Khata, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information, all of which is stored **locally on your device**:

- **Name** (Required): Your name for user profile identification
- **Email Address** (Optional): Your email address for user profile
- **Phone Number** (Optional): Your phone number for user profile
- **Profile Photo** (Optional): A photo you choose to add to your profile

**Collection Method:** All personal information is collected directly from you when you create or update your user profile. We do not collect this information automatically or from third-party sources.

### 2.2 Financial Information

We collect financial information necessary for the App's core functionality:

- Income records (amount, category, date, description)
- Expense records (amount, category, date, description)
- Debit/Credit ledger entries (khata transactions)
- Financial categories and budgets
- Zakat calculations and Islamic finance data
- Sadaqah records
- Qurbani savings
- Family expense records
- Reminders and notes related to finances

**Collection Method:** All financial information is entered by you directly into the App. We do not access your bank accounts, credit cards, or any external financial services.

### 2.3 App Settings and Preferences

We store your app preferences locally:

- Language preference
- Currency preference
- Dark mode setting
- Islamic mode setting
- App lock PIN (encrypted and hashed)
- Notification preferences

### 2.4 Subscription Information

If you purchase a Pro subscription:

- Subscription status (active/inactive)
- Subscription expiry date
- Last subscription verification timestamp

This information is verified with Google Play Billing and cached locally for offline access. Subscription data is handled according to Google Play's policies.

### 2.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:

- Device IDs or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Contacts or address book information
- Call logs or SMS messages
- Browsing history or web activity
- Biometric data (except locally stored PIN for app lock)
- Any data from other apps on your device

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

**All your data is stored locally on your device:**

- **Database:** All financial records, user profile, and app data are stored in a local SQLite database on your device
- **Profile Photos:** Stored in the app's private documents directory
- **Settings:** Stored in device's local storage (SharedPreferences)
- **App Lock PIN:** Encrypted and stored securely in local database (hashed, not plain text)
- **Backup Files:** Stored locally on your device (optional Google Drive upload if you choose)

### 4.2 Data Transmission

**We do NOT transmit your data to external servers** except in the following limited cases:

- **Google Drive Backup (Optional):** If you explicitly choose to backup to Google Drive, backup files are uploaded to your personal Google Drive account. This is completely optional and user-initiated.
- **Google Play Billing:** Subscription verification requires communication with Google Play services, but no personal or financial data is shared.
- **AdMob (Free Users Only):** If you are a free user, AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data according to Google's AdMob policies. Pro users do not see ads.

### 4.3 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data stored locally is encrypted
- **App Lock:** Optional PIN protection for your app data
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your financial data is not automatically synced to any cloud service
- **Private Storage:** All data is stored in the app's private directory, inaccessible to other apps

### 4.4 Data Retention

Your data is retained:

- **While App is Installed:** All data remains on your device as long as the app is installed
- **After Uninstall:** All data is deleted when you uninstall the app
- **Individual Records:** You can delete individual records at any time through the app

## 7. Permissions Explanation

Bonik Khata requests the following permissions:

### 7.1 Camera Permission

- **Permission:** `android.permission.CAMERA`
- **Required:** No (optional)
- **Purpose:** To take a profile photo (only when you explicitly choose to add a photo)
- **When Used:** Only when you tap to add or change your profile photo
- **Data Collected:** Profile photo (stored locally only)

### 7.2 Storage/Photos Permission

- **Permission:** `android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` / `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`
- **Required:** No (optional)
- **Purpose:** To select a profile photo from your gallery (only when you explicitly choose)
- **When Used:** Only when you choose to select a photo from your device gallery
- **Data Collected:** Profile photo (stored locally only)

### 7.3 Notifications Permission

- **Permission:** `android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS`
- **Required:** Yes (for reminder notifications)
- **Purpose:** To send you reminder notifications for financial tasks
- **When Used:** When you set up reminders in the app
- **Data Collected:** None (permission only, no data collected)

### 7.4 Exact Alarm Permission

- **Permission:** `android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`
- **Required:** Yes (for reminder notifications)
- **Purpose:** To schedule exact reminder notifications at specific times
- **When Used:** When you set up reminders with specific times
- **Data Collected:** None (permission only, no data collected)

**Important:** The App can function without camera and storage permissions. These are only needed if you want to add a profile photo.
